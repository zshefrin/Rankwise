No long-term contracts. It's month-to-month — if you want to stop, give us 30 days notice and that's it. We don't believe in trapping people. The results keep you around, not a contract. Most guys stay because they can see it working.
How much does it cost? +
Depends on your market and what you need. A solo operator in Langley is a different situation than a 10-person crew covering all of Vancouver. Book the free audit — we'll look at your specific market, show you the opportunity, and give you a straight answer on cost. No surprises.
How fast will I see results? +
Google Business Profile improvements show up within 30 days — more people seeing your listing, more clicks to your profile. Actual ranking improvements for search terms typically take 60–90 days. The full effect compounds over 6+ months. We'll give you a realistic timeline for your specific area on the audit call.
Why not just do it myself? +
Same reason homeowners hire you instead of watching a YouTube video and doing their own install. There are 40+ factors that decide where you rank — primary category, service areas, description wording, review velocity, review response cadence, photo freshness, post cadence, citation consistency across directories, backlink profile, site speed, GBP Q&A, local page content — and that's before Google's next algorithm update. Most HVAC owners who try to handle it themselves spend 5–10 hours a week for the first three months and still miss things. Your time is worth more on a job site.
Do you work with other trades or just HVAC? +
Just HVAC. That's the whole point — we're not spreading ourselves thin across dentists and landscapers and restaurants. We know how homeowners search for heating and cooling work, we know your industry, and we know the Metro Vancouver market. That focus is why it works.
Do you use AI in your work? +
Yes, the way a surgeon uses imaging — it speeds up the parts that benefit from speed, and a human makes every decision that affects your business. AI drafts first versions, tracks keyword movement across Google, and runs quality checks. A human reviews and approves every post, every report, and every response before it leaves our hands. Google's own guidance is clear: AI-generated content is fine if it's helpful, low-effort AI content is not. We operate on the helpful side of that line.
