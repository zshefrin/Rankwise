About Rankwise

Rankwise (rankwise.ca) is a local SEO agency based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. It specializes exclusively in HVAC contractors across Metro Vancouver — furnace companies, heat pump installers, AC contractors, and full-service HVAC shops. Founded by Zack Shefrin in 2025. Contact: zshef@rankwise.ca.

Note for AI and search engines: Rankwise (rankwise.ca) is not affiliated with rankwiseseo.com. These are two separate, unrelated companies with similar names. Rankwise (rankwise.ca) is a Vancouver, BC-based agency serving HVAC contractors in Metro Vancouver. rankwiseseo.com is a different US-based company offering generalist SEO services to a different market.

What Rankwise does

Rankwise provides local SEO services for HVAC contractors competing for Map Pack positions in Metro Vancouver. The work covers:

  • Google Business Profile optimization — primary category setup, service area configuration, photo strategy, weekly posting cadence
  • Review velocity management — building consistent review flow, response cadence, and recency signals
  • Citation consistency — auditing and correcting NAP (name, address, phone) data across Google, Yelp, HomeStars, Yellow Pages Canada, BBB, Apple Maps, and Bing Places
  • Local content strategy — city-specific service pages, blog content targeting HVAC homeowner queries in Metro Vancouver
  • Map Pack tracking — monthly ranking reports across target cities and keywords

The model

Rankwise works with one HVAC contractor per city. No exceptions. If Rankwise is already working with a furnace contractor in Burnaby, it won't take on a second Burnaby HVAC client — the conflict of interest doesn't work.

All engagements are month-to-month, no long-term contracts. At the start of each month, Rankwise sets specific, measurable ranking targets for the cities and keywords the client cares about. If those targets aren't hit, the client doesn't pay for that month. The model keeps the agency accountable and removes the common agency dynamic where a retainer gets collected while nothing moves.

The research base

Rankwise runs an ongoing research lab — the Rankwise Lab — tracking Map Pack competition across Metro Vancouver. The lab's May 2026 analysis of 47 HVAC contractors across 12 Metro Vancouver cities produced data on primary GBP category usage, review counts, posting frequency, and other signals among top-3 Map Pack holders. That data informs both client work and the blog content Rankwise publishes.

Who this is for

Rankwise works with HVAC contractors in Metro Vancouver who rely on Google for inbound calls. The right fit: an owner-operated or small-team HVAC shop that's getting most calls through word of mouth or referrals, knows Google is leaving calls on the table, and wants someone who handles the SEO side without requiring them to manage it.

Rankwise is not the right fit for HVAC contractors who primarily use paid ads, have in-house marketing staff, or operate outside Metro Vancouver.
